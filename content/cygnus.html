How to find Cygnus

As night falls in June or July, look east for a sparkling blue-white star, whose name is Vega. Measure with your hand about 30ยบ further east to another bright star named Deneb. Deneb is the tail of Cygnus the Swan, otherwise known as the Northern Cross.

Cygnus' Story

In ancient Greek mythology, Cygnus the Swan is associated with Queen Leda, who gave birth to two sets of twins. One set of twins was immortal because their father was Zeus (ruler of the Gods,) who transformed into a swan in order to make Queen Leda fall in love with him.
