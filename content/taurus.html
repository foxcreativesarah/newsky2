How to find Taurus

First find three bright stars in a row in the southern sky - this is Orion’s Belt. Follow the line from Orion’s Belt, about 20º with your hand, to the west to a bright star called Alderbaran. Alderbaran is the ‘Bull’s Eye’ star in the constellation, Taurus the Bull.

Taurus' Story

When Zeus, ruler of the ancient Greek Gods, fell in love with princess Europa, he transformed himself into a bull with golden horns to wisk Europa away.
